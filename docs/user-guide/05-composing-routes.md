# Composing Routes

Composing lets you build a custom route by manually selecting individual street segments
on the map.

## Starting Compose Mode

Click "Compose" in the side panel to enter compose mode. The map switches to show
individual segments (street sections between intersections) instead of full paths.

## Building Your Route

1. click a segment on the map to start your route
2. click adjacent segments to extend the route - segments must connect at intersection
   points
3. the route builds as a continuous chain from one end

If you click a segment that does not connect to your current route, you will be asked
whether to add it anyway (creating a gap in the route).

## Route Information

While composing, you can see:

- number of selected segments
- total distance of your composed route
- whether the current route forms a loop

## Undo and Clear

- **Undo** removes the last added segment
- **Clear** removes all selected segments and starts over

## Saving Your Composed Route

When you are happy with your route, click "Save" and enter a name. Composed routes are
marked as "custom" in your saved routes list.
